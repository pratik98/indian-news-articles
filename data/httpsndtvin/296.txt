The viral 'Manike Mage Mithe' Sri Lankan-singer Yohani, in an interview to NDTV, revealed that she wishes to work with many Bollywood singers, including AR Rahman. "I am a big fan of Bollywood music. A lot of music in Sri Lanka is inspired by Bollywood," she said. Apart from AR Rahman, Yohani named Honey Singh, Divine and Neha Kakkar as well.
