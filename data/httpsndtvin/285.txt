Actor and filmmaker John Krasinski who starred in the hit horror film A Quiet Place in 2018 has directed the sequel of the film A Quiet Place, which also stars his wife, actress Emily Blunt. The film releases in India on October 8, 2021, after many delays due to the pandemic. NDTV's Rohit Khilnani interviewed John before the Covid19 pandemic and got him talking about the film and his wife Emily Blunt.
