5 Unique Idli Recipes That Are A Must-Try

When relishing South Indian food, idlis are definitely the first choice of many. After all the fluffy and light idlis are comforting and go well with sambhar, rasam and all types of chutneys. Over the years, the popularity of this dish has risen so much that many of us have experimented with its taste and created never-ending yummy varieties! So, if you also want to indulge in them, check out these yummy idli recipes!
